About Me

Hello Dear, I am Redoyan Hossen. Currently, I am pursuing a B.Sc. in Multimedia and Creative Technology at Daffodil International University in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I completed my Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) from Ramganj Model University College, Lakshmipur, Chittagong, Bangladesh. I am a professional Graphic Designer, Motion Designer, and UI/UX Designer with about two years of experience. I have worked with various organizations and companies, creating designs for business products, YouTube channels, and other digital needs. I continue to work actively in these fields, collaborating with different people and businesses. My research interests include UI/UX design, Motion Graphics, and 2D and 3D animation. I am passionate about combining creativity with technology to create engaging and user-friendly designs. Alongside my professional work, I also enjoy hobbies like photography and generating innovative design ideas, which inspire my creative process. My future goal is to become a top-level UI/UX and Motion Graphic Designer, continually improving my skills and contributing to impactful digital experiences. I am committed to learning and growing in the creative industry, aiming to work on international projects and with renowned brands in the coming years. Thank you.
